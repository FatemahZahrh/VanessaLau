ABOUT VANESSA

In 2018, after climbing the corporate ladder at a Fortune 500 company, and hating it, I took the leap of faith and quit. After saying goodbye to my prestigious cubicle, I found myself back at square one: working minimum wage as a coffee barista.

While serving lattes and mastering the art of small talk, I started sharing social media strategies online. When my tips started gaining popularity on the internet, I began offering online services and digital courses.

IN LESS THAN A YEAR, I HAD ALREADY BUILT A 6 FIGURE BUSINESS.

Today, my team has grown and we run a multi-million dollar company dedicated to helping corporate escapees, content creators, and new coaches expand their reach and build incredible businesses using social media, just like I did.

Our mission is to open the door to life- changing opportunities and prove that it’s possible - and fun! - to build a wildly successful digital career that offers freedom, income and impact.​

WHAT WE CARE ABOUT

BELIEF #1

We take extreme ownership of our success.

This belief also carries over to how we empower our clients and community, whether it’s through our content, interactions, and programs. To us, quality and focus matters. Our goal is to create an environment where leaders walk the talk, take action, and own their results, 100%.

BELIEF #2

We stay involved.

We deeply care about our customers, team mates, audience members, and our world community. We always do our very best to make sure our people feel seen, respected, and heard - even at scale. We aim to leave lasting impressions and experiences with the impact we make.

BELIEF #3

There is no failure, only feedback.

We know that any feedback is good feedback because that is the only way we can grow. Collecting as much data as we can from each other and those around us allows us to make sound decisions in business and in life. We don’t take our work personally and we know that there is always room to do better, and be better.

BELIEF #4

Inclusivity matters.

Our company continuously reflects and invests in resources to widen our lens to be more inclusive to other races, sexual orientations, ages, and ability statuses as we run our programs, support our students, and do business. Our top priority is for our clients and community to feel seen, appreciated, and respected. We continue to learn in public, and are always working towards using our platform, time, and resources to create even more inclusivity inside and outside our programs.
